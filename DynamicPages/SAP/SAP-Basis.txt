Brief Job Advertisement for SAP Basis Developer at SkillCapital

SkillCapital is seeking a highly skilled and experienced SAP Basis Developer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong understanding of SAP Basis administration and a proven track record of success in implementing and maintaining SAP systems.

Roles and Responsibilities for SAP Basis Developer

1. Manage and maintain SAP systems, including hardware, software, and network infrastructure.
2. Perform system upgrades, migrations, and data conversions.
3. Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues related to SAP systems.
4. Provide technical support to SAP users and administrators.
5. Monitor system performance and implement performance improvements.
6. Stay up-to-date with the latest SAP Basis technologies and best practices.

Job Requirements for SAP Basis Developer

1. Minimum experience of 3 years or a highly skilled fresher in SAP Basis administration.
2. Strong understanding of SAP Basis architecture and components.
3. Experience with SAP HANA, SAP NetWeaver, and SAP Solution Manager.
4. Knowledge of operating systems, databases, and networking.
5. Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
6. Strong communication and teamwork skills.

Preferred Skills for SAP Basis Developer

1. Experience with SAP S/4HANA.
2. Knowledge of cloud computing and virtualization technologies.
3. Experience with SAP security and compliance.
4. Experience with SAP automation tools and scripts.
5. Knowledge of SAP Fiori and SAPUI5.
6. Experience with SAP HANA Live.
