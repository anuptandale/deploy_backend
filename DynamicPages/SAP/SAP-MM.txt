Brief Job Advertisement for SAP MM Developer at SkillCapital

SkillCapital, a leading provider of SAP solutions, is seeking a highly skilled and experienced SAP MM Developer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong understanding of SAP MM modules and a proven track record of delivering successful projects.

Preferred Skills for SAP MM Developer

1. Expertise in SAP MM modules, including purchasing, inventory management, and material master data.
2. Strong knowledge of SAP ERP systems and related technologies.
3. Experience in customizing and configuring SAP MM to meet specific business requirements.
4. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
5. Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
6. Problem-solving and analytical skills.

Job Requirements for SAP MM Developer

1. Minimum experience of 3 years or a highly skilled fresher in SAP MM.
2. Strong understanding of SAP MM processes and best practices.
3. Experience in implementing and supporting SAP MM solutions.
4. Ability to work with cross-functional teams, including finance, logistics, and IT.
5. Excellent communication and presentation skills.
6. Ability to travel occasionally.

Roles and Responsibilities for SAP MM Developer

1. Develop and maintain SAP MM configurations to meet business requirements.
2. Manage master data, including materials, vendors, and purchasing info records.
3. Process purchase orders, goods receipts, and invoices.
4. Monitor inventory levels and manage stock transfers.
5. Conduct variance analysis and identify cost-saving opportunities.
6. Provide support to users and resolve SAP MM-related issues.
